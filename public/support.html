Frequently Asked Questions

How does fit scoring work?
Each account is scored on three dimensions: ICP Alignment (does this company match your target profile?), Customer Similarity (how close to your existing wins?), and Competitive Landscape (what vendor do they use today?). Scores are deterministic — same inputs, same score every time.
Can I edit the research brief?
Every field in the brief is editable. Click any text to modify it. Your corrections are tracked and flow into all downstream output — hypothesis, discovery questions, coaching, and CRM push.
Where does the data come from?
Every brief uses live web research — not cached training data. Executives, financials, hiring signals, and competitive positioning are all sourced from real-time search. We also layer in 31 proprietary knowledge modules covering 15+ industries and 10+ sales frameworks.
Is the AI making things up?
We've invested heavily in anti-hallucination guardrails: web-grounded research, citation verification, golden-set regression testing across 25 sentinel companies, and code-level filters. When we can't verify something, we say so. That said — always verify executive names and key facts before a live call.
How do I connect HubSpot?
Go to Settings (click your avatar or the gear icon) → HubSpot section → Connect. You'll authorize via HubSpot's OAuth flow. Once connected, "Push to HubSpot" appears in your brief action bar. One click pushes the company record, a rich session note, and optionally a deal and executive contacts.
What's included in the free trial?
3 full runs — each run includes the complete 9-step workflow: ICP, prospect lists, fit scoring, research brief, call prep, live call coaching, post-call analysis, and CRM push. No credit card required.

Do you use my data to train AI models?
No. Your session data, briefs, and ICP configurations are never used to train AI models. Our AI provider (Anthropic) processes prompts in real time and discards them per their commercial terms. See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.
