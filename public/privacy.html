Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Cambrian Catalyst LLC ("we", "us") operates the Cambrian Catalyst sales intelligence platform at cambriancatalyst.ai. We are based in Seattle, WA.

2. What We Collect

We collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Session data: seller URLs, ICP configurations, target accounts, briefs, and notes you create
  • Usage data: API calls, token consumption, feature usage, and session timestamps
  • Technical data: IP address (for rate limiting), browser type (from headers)

3. How We Use It

  • Provide and improve the Service (brief generation, scoring, coaching)
  • Track usage for billing and token limits
  • Send transactional emails (password reset, invitations)
  • Aggregate anonymized usage patterns to improve our knowledge layers

4. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your session content to train AI models
  • We do not share your ICP or account data with other users
  • We do not display ads

5. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase (authentication and database) — your data is stored in their SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
  • Anthropic (AI model provider) — prompts are sent for processing but not stored or used for training per their enterprise terms
  • Vercel (hosting) — serves the application and API endpoints
  • Google Workspace (email) — transactional emails only

6. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • JWT-based authentication with cryptographic signature verification
  • Row-level security (RLS) isolating organization data
  • HTTPS encryption in transit (HSTS enforced)
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Rate limiting on all API endpoints
  • Proprietary knowledge layer data served only to authenticated users, never included in the client bundle

7. Data Retention

Your session data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual sessions from the app. If you cancel your account, we will delete your data within 30 days upon request.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access, export, or delete your data by contacting us
  • Close your account at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

9. Cookies

We use sessionStorage (not cookies) for authentication tokens. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising pixels.

10. Children

The Service is not intended for anyone under 18.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice.

12. Contact

Privacy questions? Email info@cambriancatalyst.com.
