# Cambrian Catalyst — Value Proposition

**Version:** June 2026

---

## The Problem

Sales reps spend 65% of their time on non-selling activities. The #1 time sink: research and preparation. A typical AE spends 30-90 minutes researching a company before a first call — and most of that research is surface-level (LinkedIn, company website, maybe a 10-K skim).

The result: reps walk into conversations underprepared, ask generic questions, pitch capabilities that don't match, and lose deals to competitors who did the homework.

**The cost of bad prep:**
- Longer sales cycles (prospects don't feel understood)
- Lower win rates (generic pitches don't convert)
- Higher rep burnout (more activity, less results)
- Lost credibility (one wrong fact kills trust permanently)

---

## The Solution

Cambrian Catalyst replaces 60-90 minutes of manual research with 30 seconds of AI-powered intelligence. But it's not just faster research — it's *better* research. Things a human couldn't find in 90 minutes:

- What the prospect's CEO said on their last earnings call
- That they're hiring 5 data engineers (signal: building infrastructure)
- That their competitor just won a contract renewal (displacement opportunity)
- That their Glassdoor reviews mention "below-market compensation" (retention risk = buying trigger)
- That your customer DigniFi has a similar profile and achieved 70% lift (proof point)

---

## Three Promises

### 1. Every rep walks in as the most prepared person in the room
19 sections of intelligence — not Wikipedia summaries, but actionable sales intel. Strategic analysis, buying signals, competitive positioning, financial trends, hiring patterns, market sentiment, and a tailored approach for THIS seller selling to THIS buyer.

### 2. Every pitch is grounded in what you actually sell
The brief maps YOUR specific products to THEIR specific needs. It cites YOUR case studies, YOUR proof points, YOUR customers. It never suggests capabilities you don't have (exclusions list prevents it). A rep who pitches something their company doesn't do loses credibility — Cambrian prevents that.

### 3. Every output gets smarter as you use it
Edit a field, upload a doc, add a proof point — every correction feeds into downstream outputs. The discovery questions reference the brief. The hypothesis references the ICP. The post-call routing references the discovery capture. It's a compounding intelligence loop, not isolated AI calls.
