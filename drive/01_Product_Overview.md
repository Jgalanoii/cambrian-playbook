# Cambrian Catalyst — Product Overview

**Version:** June 2026 | **Status:** Private Beta | **URL:** cambriancatalyst.ai
**Founded by:** Joe Galano | Cambrian Catalyst LLC | West Seattle, WA

---

## What It Is

Cambrian Catalyst is a **Sales Intelligence Platform** that researches companies, maps your products to their needs, and arms sellers with the exact intel to close deals. Every output is grounded in live web research, tailored to what the seller actually sells, and verified against 45 proprietary knowledge layers spanning 37 industries.

It is NOT a CRM, not a dialer, not a sequencer. It's the intelligence layer that sits upstream of all of those — making every downstream tool and conversation more effective.

---

## Who It's For

- **Primary users:** Account Executives, SDRs, Solution Architects, Revenue leaders, GTM consultants
- **Company profile:** B2B SaaS, FinTech, PayTech, InsurTech, HealthTech, enterprise software, professional services
- **Company size:** Series B+ benefits most, but works for any stage
- **Wedge ICP:** Boutique GTM consultants and fractional revenue leaders advising 3-10 companies simultaneously

## The 10-Step Pipeline

| Step | Name | What Happens |
|------|------|-------------|
| 0 | **START** | Seller URL, product page scan, doc upload, proof points, exclusions, targeting preferences |
| 1 | **ICP & RFPs** | 2-pass ICP build (deep research + structured format), live RFP search across SAM.gov/state portals/commercial, competitive intel |
| 2 | **IMPORT** | CSV upload, manual entry, or AI-generated target list (150 accounts across 37 industries) |
| 3 | **FIT SCORES** | 3-dimension scoring: Product/Service Fit (45pts), Customer Lookalike (30pts), Competitive Displacement (25pts) |
| 4 | **ACCOUNTS** | Ranked prospect table, competitor detection (flagged at 5%), known customer override, bulk HubSpot push |
| 5 | **BRIEF** | 10 parallel micro-calls producing 19 sections across 3 groups (~30-45 seconds) |
| 6 | **PREP** | RIVER hypothesis framework, 5 discovery questions, talk tracks, objection handling |
| 7 | **LIVE CALL** | Real-time AI coaching (Milton), structured gate questions, discovery capture |
| 8 | **SOLUTION FIT** | SA review, buyer readiness assessment, architecture gaps, implementation roadmap |
| 9 | **POST-CALL** | Deal routing (Fast Track / Nurture / Disqualify), CRM push, follow-up email draft |

## The Brief — 19 Sections, 3 Groups

### Your Angle (how to approach this prospect)
1. **Elevator Pitch** — 45-second spoken pitch, human tone, grounded in research
2. **Strategic Analysis** — what's driving the account + why seller is positioned to win
3. **Opening Angle** — 1-2 sentences that make an executive stop scrolling
4. **Outreach Emails** — 2 ready-to-send variants (curious + insight style)
5. **How You Help** — product-to-need mapping, champion intel, case studies, teaching insights

### Company Intelligence (who they are)
6. **Company Overview** — snapshot, revenue, ownership, employees, HQ, funding
7. **Key Executives** — name, title, background, engagement angle
8. **Recent Headlines** — press releases, news, with relevance
9. **Buying Signals** — growth signals + recent triggers
10. **Open Positions** — hiring patterns with strategic interpretation
11. **Market Sentiment** — Glassdoor, G2, NPS, standout reviews, sales angle
12. **Tech Stack** — CRM, ERP, HRIS, payments, analytics, infrastructure
13. **Culture & Workforce** — values, style, decision-making, incumbent vendors
14. **Financial Intelligence** — revenue trends, margins, segments, capital priorities, earnings guidance
15. **Competitive Positioning** — market position, competitors, where they win/lose, displacement angle
16. **Board & Investors** — board members, lead investors, thesis, mandate

### Deal Readiness (what to do next)
17. **Watch-Outs** — procurement risks, incumbent lock-in, credibility gaps
18. **Approval Gate Map** — seller gates + buyer gates + critical path + this week's priority
19. **5 Questions to Ask** — grounded in research, sequenced from rapport-building to insight-revealing

+ **Quick Take** (TL;DR) — top finding, opportunity, risk — first thing the user sees
